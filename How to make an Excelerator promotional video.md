# Recording the video using Snagit

  
 

1\. Go to the start menu and open up Snagit.

2\. Open up Excelerator on Excel.

3\. Have Sage 200 opened so you switch back and forth whilst recording.

4\. Unpin the blue connection bar. See image below.

![](images/Sales_Sales_Wiki_Images_Blue_Hyper_name_bar.png)  
 

5\. Ensure you have the full screen caputured ready to be recorded.

6\. Press the record button to start recording.

7\. Once you have finished recording, click the finish button on Snagit.

8\. Cut the bits you don't need by using the Snagit Editor.

9\. Save as a MP4 ready to be imported into Adobe Premiere Pro.

  
 

**Helpful Tips:**

\- Do not record the video all in one go. Take your time, and make things easier for yourself by recording the video in 2 parts.

\- If the Excelerator buffers or freezes, don't worry, you can always edit that bit out using the Snagit Editor.

\- Do not move the mouse curser too fast or too slow, take your time. Remember that the viewer of the video may be watching the product in action for the first time.

\- Try to keep the video around 3 minutes long. 

  
 

# Video assets\- Images, Sounds \& Music

  
 

The video assets will be the additional branding that we will need to add into Premiere Pro to make the video look more professional. 

You will be able to find these in the Digital Marketing folder on the OneDrive.

The file path is **Wiki Page \> Office 365 \> OneDrive \> Shared \> Digital Marketing \> Videos \> Video Assets**

You may have to ask Raghu to give you access to the Digital Marketing folder in case you do not have access.

The files that you will be looking for are:

**\- Codis Logo Screen** (This will be the logo screen that will be at the start of the video)

**\- Animated Intro.mp4** (This will come straight after the logo screen, this intro is a moving image of the Codis Logo)

**\- Intro Ping Sound** (This 'ping' sound should be in sync with the moving image of the Codis Logo animiated intro.

**\-** **YouTubeScreen.png** (This image will come near the end of the video once you have finished presenting Excelerator)

**\- URLScreen.png** (The URL image is 'codis.co.uk', and this should be presented at the end of the video. Approximately 10 seconds long)

**\- Taskbar\- 2018\.ai** (This is a thin faded strip that presents the codis URL. Should be placed at the very bottom of the video, throughout the video.

**\- Background Music** (There are several you can choose from, personally I would choose the 'Happiness' track. But this is up to you)

  
 

Download the above files and save them in a folder on your desktop or somewhere in File Explorer.

  
 

# Using Adobe Premiere Pro CC

  
 

Once you have all the recordings, video assets \& images, you are ready to import them into Premiere Pro. First thing you need to do is open up Adobe Premiere Pro.

\- Start up a new project.

\- You will be asked to the save the project.

In Premiere Pro you will see 4 panels.

**1\. Project Panel:** Import and organise the video clips that you will use in your project.

**2\. Timeline Panel:** Arrange and edit your video clips to build your actual project.

**3\. Source Monitor:** Review the video clips you have imported into your project.

**4\. Program Monitor:** Preview your final project.

(If you are new to Adobe Premiere Pro then it is highly recommended that you go through the tutorials within the program.)

\- Drag or copy the files into the Project Panel.

\- Once you have all the media files that you require, you can start adding them into the timeline.
