Excelerator has multiple versions which work with different versions of Excel, Sage, Windows and CPU architecture (32 or 64 bit). 

## 

## Office 365

There are two versions of [Office 365](https://products.office.com/en-gb/office-365-home) \- on premise and online. 

On Premise 

We think that apart from the Microsoft's licencing, this is identical to regular Office 2013\. Excelerator is supported for this version as it is for Office 2013\. 

Online 

This is a version that runs in a browser, not on your local PC. Excelerator does not yet support this version.
