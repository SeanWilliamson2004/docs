**Billing** is the process of Invoicing out and collecting payments from Codis customers. It is managed by the **Accounts** and [**Sales**](http://intranet/pages/new?title=sales%20department) departments.Sales department processes New orders and Accounts department processes Renewals.  

## Process

### Subscriptions

The billing process is split into two main stages.Preparing New Orders and Preparing Renewals.

#### New Orders

Sales department receives Purchase Order(PO) form the customer or reseller, which is added to Sage 50 as New Sales order (quotation).New Sales order (quotation) should reflect the same description and amount as in PO. 

1. Login to Sage 50
2. Click on '' **Quotations**'' and click on '**New**' Tab.
3. Add account name **''A/C''** from the drop down list. If the account is not there in the list then you might need to add customer in Sage 50\.
4. Click on the first line under **'Product code'** and select the module from drop down list.
5. Click on the second line and add Licence \& Maintenance(L\&M) part for the same module. For Example if the Product is ''Excelerator NL Journal'' then we have to add ''Excelerator NL Journal Licence and Maintenance'' too.
6. Under Description \> Details \>Comment
1. **Comment 1**\- Add cover period, normally one year for L\&M. For Ex (20/10/15 to 19/10/16\).
2. **Comment 2** \- Add End User name if the order is from the Reseller.
3. **Order Reference**\- Add Licence and Maintenance Expiry Date.
4. **Quantity**\- As on Purchase Order.
5. **Unit Price**\- Normally set on Sage 50\.(If the Order is via reseller the price may vary)

8. Order Details \-
1. **Delivery Address**\- Add Payment Terms.
2. **Customer Order Reference**\- Add ''Purchase Order Number''.

10. Once you satisfied with the Sales Order(quotation) Click ''**Save**'' then click ''**Complete**'' to generate an Invoice.

#### Renewal

Renewal Invoice is an annual Invoice for all the Licenses previously purchased as well as the new ones purchases in the mid term. All the customer accounts have their own alignment date. All their Licenses are set to expire on the same date, any new purchase made in between the year will be aligned on renewal Invoice. We can find the billable report on CRM. 1. Step 1: Prepare the Sales orders.
1. Login to Sage 50\.
2. Add account name ''A/C'' from the drop down list.
3. Click on the first line under 'Product code' and select the module Licence and Maintenance from drop down list.
4. Under Description \> Details \>Comment
1. Comment 1\- Add cover period as per their alignment date also Align any new purchase made in within the year.
2. Comment 2 \- Add Original Order number.
3. Order Reference\- Add Licence and Maintenance Expiry Date.

#### Invoices

The Document /Bill sent to a Reseller or a Customer (buyer) with details of the product and the cost. 1. Login to Sage 50\.
2. Double check the '**Sales order**'.
3. If you are happy with the Sales order, Click the ''**Complete**'' tab at the bottom.
4. Once the Sales order is completed, Invoice is generated on the Invoice Screen.
5. Print the Invoice for Accounts records purpose and send an email copy to the Buyer.

### CRM

A video demonstration on how to process purchase orders via CRM.  

Follow these steps: 

1\) Type Company name into the' **Company**' search field on the CRM interface. 

2\) Click on the company 

3\) Right click on **'New'** which is located on the left side of the screen 

4\) Click **'Order'** which is listed on the drop down list. 

5\) Add the Module and quantity in the  **'Description'** field. For example: 12 X PO Receipt. 

6\) Add the Purchase Order number into the **'PO Reference'** field. The purchase order number will be noted on the purchase order. 

7\) Click the **'Next'** button which is located on the right side of the screen. 

8\) Add **'Excelerator'** in the **'Product Family'** field along with the sage type and module version. For example: Excelerator Standard sage 1000 

9\) Add the module in the **'Product'** Field for example: PO Receipt 1000 ST. 

10\) Add the net amount on the invoice in the **'Quoted Price'** field. 

11\) Add the number of modules requested in the **'Quantity'** field for example: If the customer has selected 12 PO Receipt modules then put '12' in the **'Quantity'** field. 

12\) Click the **'Save'** button which is on the right side of the screen. 

13\) After you have saved the purchase order you need to make note of the **SO Reference** number which will be in the **'SO Reference'** field.
