Codis has a referral network \- anyone who refers business to us, if registered as a lead, earns a 10% commission  

On our [Codis Referral Network](https://www.codis.co.uk/codis-referral-network) web page, the messaging is: "Earn a 10% commission for every new customer you refer who adopts Sage 200\. Recommend Sage 200 to businesses in your network and get paid when they invest in their first year's subscription"

This aligns and integrates with the Sage Referral Network \- [sage.com/en\-gb/cp/referralnetwork](https://www.sage.com/en-gb/cp/referralnetwork/)  

Benefits to the customer

1. commercial: receive 10% commission \- which they get as a cash benefit, or can choose to have amount discounted from their Sage 200 support
2. relationship/reputational: referring trusted vendors can strengthen a professional’s role as a strategic advisor

Benefits to Codis  

1. lead generation: more qualified sales opportunities
2. commercial: potential referral revenue \- if the referrer doesn't want it / can't accept it \- that we can then reallocate to the new customer and/or apply a discount to the referring customer
3. reputational: stronger position as a trusted advisor

Process to get more leads referred to Codis

1. Identify who would make a great referral partner \- anyone who can think of other businesses who would benefit from Sage 200
- these could be our direct customers, our suppliers, our customers' suppliers (e.g. their IT companies) etc.
- note these in the [referral network contacts spreadsheet](/:x:/s/Sales/IQA9wXCKgOb7T40DES9dnDQNAQnBwNb9jmWjYy8nmiWrcvk?e=eNYv3r)

4. Make contact with them, talking about the benefits of referring business to Codis, arrange to walk them through process of how to register as a referrer and to register leads
5. Walk them through how to join the Sage Referral Network at [sage.com/en\-gb/cp/referralnetwork](https://www.sage.com/en-gb/cp/referralnetwork/)
6. Walk them through how they can submit each introduction at [get.sage.com/SageReferralNetwork](https://get.sage.com/SageReferralNetwork) \- ensuring they mention Codis Ltd under "If you have a preference as to who will contact the customer and work with them to adopt the new Sage solution, please include this below."
7. that's it \- led the leads flow in
