The 'Document Drop' option is available to attach important documents such as Purchase Orders, Signed Sales Orders, End User License Agreements(EULAs),Quotations and Invoices. 

## The Document Drop

1\) Firstly you need to select the correct Account 

2\) You need to select the right opportunity under the'Opportunity' tab where you want to file the document. 

3\) You then need to right click the Attachment that you wish to save in CRM, and then click 'Copy'. 

4\) You then need to select the correct opportunity which you will find by clicking the 'Opportunity' tab. 

5\) Once you are in the opportunity, you then need to click on the 'Documents' tab at the top. 

6\) When you are under the Documents section, you then need to right click on the 'Document Drop' icon which is located on the right side of the page. 

7\) Click 'Paste' 

8\) You need to select the right document type under the 'Type' drop down menu. 

9\) Remember to add additional details in the 'Details' box. 

10\) Click the 'Save' button.
