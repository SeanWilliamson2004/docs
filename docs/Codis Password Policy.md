**Policy Number: PWD\-002**  

**Effective Date: 01\.04\.2023**  

## 1\. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines and procedures for the maintenance of passwords used to access the company's information systems, with the implementation of a password manager such as [**1Password**](1Password.md). This policy aims to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of sensitive data, protect against unauthorized access, and streamline password management processes.

## 2\. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, and other personnel who have access to the company's information systems and resources. It encompasses all computing devices, network accounts, applications, and databases owned, operated, or controlled by the company.

## 3\. Password Manager Implementation

3\.1\. The company shall implement a password manager solution, such as [**1Password**](1Password.md), to facilitate secure storage and management of all system passwords.

3\.2\. The password manager shall be used to generate and store complex passwords that adhere to the company's password complexity requirements (as outlined below).  

Generate the passwords from [**Password Generator**](https://1password.com/password-generator/) (Strictly for 1Password Master Password) and then either from 1Password app or its suggested passwords from browser extension.

3\.3\. Access to the password manager shall be provided only to authorized personnel based on their job responsibilities and access requirements.

## 4\. Password Creation and Storage

4\.1\. Employees are encouraged to use the password manager's built\-in password generator to create strong and unique passwords for each system or account they access.

4\.2\. Passwords must not be shared with others, and users should not attempt to memorize passwords manually.

4\.3\. All passwords must be stored and managed exclusively within the password manager application.

## 5\. Multi\-Factor Authentication (MFA) Integration

5\.1\. Wherever possible, the password manager shall be integrated with multi\-factor authentication (MFA) for an additional layer of security.

5\.2\. MFA should be enabled for accessing the password manager itself and for any systems or applications that support MFA.

## 6\. Password Rotation and Management

6\.1\. Passwords shall be rotated regularly, following the password rotation schedule defined by the company (e.g., every 90 days), or in compliance with any specific requirements defined for certain systems.

6\.2\. Users shall promptly update passwords within the password manager if they are involved in a security incident or if they suspect any unauthorized access to their accounts.

## 7\. Prohibited Password Practices

7\.1\. Sharing Passwords: Sharing passwords with colleagues, friends, family members, or any unauthorized individuals is strictly prohibited.

7\.2\. Writing Down Passwords: Writing down passwords on sticky notes, notepads, or any easily accessible medium is not allowed.

7\.3\. Using Default Passwords: The use of default passwords or easily guessable passwords is strictly forbidden.

7\.4\. Password Reuse: Passwords used for personal accounts should not be reused for work\-related accounts, and vice versa.

## 8\. Lost or Stolen Password Manager Access

8\.1\. In case of a lost or stolen device containing access to the password manager, employees must immediately report the incident to the IT department.

8\.2\. The IT department shall take necessary actions to revoke access to the password manager from the lost or stolen device and initiate appropriate security measures.

## 9\. Training and Awareness

9\.1\. Regular training and awareness programs shall be conducted to educate employees and users about password manager best practices, potential risks, and the importance of maintaining strong passwords.

## 10\. Review and Revision

This policy will be reviewed annually by the IT department and may be updated or revised as needed to address changes in technology, industry standards, or regulatory requirements.

## 11\. Policy Acknowledgment

By accessing the company's systems and resources, all users agree to comply with this password maintenance policy and acknowledge their understanding of the importance of password security and confidentiality.
