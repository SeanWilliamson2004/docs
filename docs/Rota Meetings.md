Rota meetings are held weekly by the support team. The purpose is to plan and review. Plan for two weeks in advance and review how the team performed last week. 

## Rota

The Rota is an excel sheet consisting of a matrix days of the week by Consultant. Screen shot 

## Support Cases

All outstanding support cases are reviewed and solutions discussed. This is usually done by reviewing open cases to the Support Team. Screen Shot 

## Consultancy Cases

A report is prepared from CRM \- Reports \- Favourite Reports \- Consultancy2 \-   
Display Options \- set to XLS   
Cases Status set to Open Screen Shot The report is opened in Excel and copied into the Rota. Screen shot
