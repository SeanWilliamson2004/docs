Online help videos are ... 

## Creation

GIFs can be created with [LiceCap](http://www.cockos.com/licecap/how_to_licecap.gif). 

### GIF standards

These are the standards to be applied for creating GIF images. 

1. Turn the speed of the Mouse down low – control pane
2. Move the mouse in straight lines
3. Display in animation – Mouse button press – shows where the mouse is clicked.
4. When shooting Excel ensure the whole Excel page fits inside the Video shot.
5. Ensure Codis Templates (branding logo) are open if shooting Excelerator clips.
6. Length should not be greater than 30 seconds
7. Naming Convention – Folder Location
8. Click the image– to open the image in the original size
