## **Login URL**

[**https://codis.1password.com/**](https://codis.1password.com/)  

## **Overview**

1Password is a place to store all of your passwords. You access 1Password using a **master password**. 

Please refer [**Codis Password Policy**](Codis Password Policy.md) and **must:**  

1. Generate a strong **master password** using the steps below (see Get Started). Write it down on paper, memorise, and shred/tear the paper after few days. Use this to access 1password.
2. Generate **random passwords** for everything else (eg your crm, codis email address, etc) and store it in 1password. They must be length 19 and include numbers and symbols.

**Never create a password yourself** (including your master password), **always generate** it using a password generator. Passwords created by humans are not random and can be guessed by robots.  

Your master password should be the only password that you ever need to memorise. **Never store any password outside of 1pass, or share any password via email or microsoft teams to anyone inside or outside the company**.   
## **Password File Syncing**

1Password can be configured through 1Password.com, a paid subscription\-based server sync service maintained by the developers. Travel Mode feature for subscribers of 1Password.com, enables omission of password entries not tagged as safe for travel from the local storage on a particular device, reducing the impact of being obliged by officials to unlock access at country border crossings.  
## **Browser Extensions**

1Password integrates with desktop web browsers including Safari, Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Opera. The extension can remember logins for websites, fill in website logins automatically, and generate random passwords for new websites. To use browser extensions, the user must have administrative rights on the computer where the browser is installed. On the mobile side, 1Password offers integration with browsers and apps on iOS and Android devices using various methods.

## **Get Started**

Please follow below steps

**1**. Enable your 1Password account by following below steps

a) Open 1Password invitation email and click on **Join Now**

![](images/IT_IT_Wiki_Images_1Password_Join_1Password_-_Email_Invitation.jpg)   

b) It would open your web browser. Follow through the steps, **verify your email address** and then set up your 1Password master password by generating it through this link [**Password Generator**](https://1password.com/password-generator/) and keeping settings as **Memorable Password, Full Words and Length 4** as shown below.  

**Note:** Please note down your master password on a piece of paper until you memorise it, then destroy it afterwards.

![](images/IT_IT_Wiki_Images_1Password_1Password_Master_Password_Policy.jpg)   

 c) If it generates your 1Password Emergency Kit PDF file with your secret key (as shown below), **DO NOT** download that pdf on your PC and click "**I've already saved it**".

![](images/IT_IT_Wiki_Images_1Password_Emergency_Kit.jpg)  

**2\.** Download and install 1Password for Windows (Requires Windows 11 64\-bit or Windows 10 64\-bit) \- [**https://1password.com/downloads/windows/**](https://1password.com/downloads/windows/)

**3\.** Install Browser Extension (Supports Chrome, Brave, Firefox, Edge, and Safari) \- [**https://1password.com/downloads/browser\-extension/**](https://1password.com/downloads/browser-extension/)

**4\.** Save your first password

- Sign in to your favorite website.
- 1Password will ask to save your username and password.
- Click Save Login.

## **Set Up 1Password On Another Device or Browser**

To access your 1Password account on another device or new web browser, you would need to configure it once before you could logon with your 1password master password.  

To configure 1Password you need Email, Secret Key and 1Password master password. **On devices with camera** like mobile and laptop, you may scan the **SETUP CODE** and it will pick your email along with the secret key.  

Please find below ways to access your secret key without Emergency Kit or saving/writing it anywhere.   

### **To access Secret Key through web browser where you activated your 1Password account initially**.

## **Change 1Password Master Password**

If you need to change your 1Password master password, follow below steps.  
- Logon to your 1Password account through the web browser by clicking [this url](https://codis.1password.com/) and using your existing old master password.
- On right hand side top corner, click on small arrow pointing downwards next to your name. Click **My Profile** and then click **Change Password** as shown below.

![](images/IT_IT_Wiki_Images_1Password_1Password_-_Change_Password_01.jpg)  

- Use [**Password Generator**](https://1password.com/password-generator/) to generate a new password keeping settings as **Memorable Password, Full Words and Length 4**. Click **Copy Secure Password**
- Specify your existing old 1Password master password in Current Password and then paste and confirm the new secure password copied in previous step. Click on **Change Password** to save the new password as shown below.

![](images/IT_IT_Wiki_Images_1Password_1Password_-_Change_Password_02.jpg)  
- Logon to 1Password with your new master password.
