## What Sage 200 reports does Intellerator replace?

Here is the sage documentation for the reports that Intellerator is meant to replace. They might have a few words as to what the values mean and/or why the user would want that report.

1. Nominal transactions dashboard: [https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/Content/NL/Prepare\_Nominal\_Account\_Reports.htm](https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/Content/NL/Prepare_Nominal_Account_Reports.htm "https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/content/nl/prepare_nominal_account_reports.htm")  

Most of the reports in the Account Analysis section here can be replaced with the dashboard
2. Financial statements: same with the financial statement section of the above link. Currently there is no cash flow forecast however.
3. SOP: [https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/Content/SOP/Prepare\_Sales\_Orders\_Reports.htm](https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/Content/SOP/Prepare_Sales_Orders_Reports.htm "https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/content/sop/prepare_sales_orders_reports.htm")  

Sales orders report (I can't find where in Sage you can view a report of orders by product, might be in another module)
4. POP: [https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/Content/POP/Prepare\_Purchase\_Order\_Reports.htm](https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/Content/POP/Prepare_Purchase_Order_Reports.htm "https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/content/pop/prepare_purchase_order_reports.htm")  

Orders, order lines, returns, return lines
5. Credit control:  
[https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/Content/SL/Prepare\_Customer\_Account\_Reports.htm\#Creditcontrol](https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/Content/SL/Prepare_Customer_Account_Reports.htm#Creditcontrol "https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/content/sl/prepare_customer_account_reports.htm#creditcontrol")  

Mainly the Aged Debtors reports but also Due Date report
6. Payments Control: [https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/Content/PL/Prepare\_Supplier\_Account\_Reports.htm\#Paymentscontrol](https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/Content/PL/Prepare_Supplier_Account_Reports.htm#Paymentscontrol "https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/content/pl/prepare_supplier_account_reports.htm#paymentscontrol")  

Mainly aged creditors and due date report
7. Stock valuation report: [https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/Content/SC/Stock%20valuation.htm](https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/Content/SC/Stock%20valuation.htm "https://desktophelp.sage.co.uk/sage200/professional/content/sc/stock%20valuation.htm")
