This video explains how to correctly transfer licences between Terminal/Citrix and Standard.

The following basic steps applies:

1\. Cancel the licences within Licensing in CRM. Follow heading 1\.2 on [Excelerator Cancellation Process](Excelerator Cancellation Process.md)

2\. Go into the Customer account \> Opportunity \> Orders \> Add a new order \> Add the correct licences.

3\. Activate the licences in Licensing \> renew the licence with the correct alignment date.
