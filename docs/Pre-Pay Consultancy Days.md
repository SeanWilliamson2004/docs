When a customer requests help with something outside standard support but too small for a formal project, offer Pre\-Pay Consultancy Days.

Explain that this allows them to pre\-purchase consultancy time and draw down in 1\-hour increments for minor system changes, training, or enhancements \- without the usual half\-day minimum charge.

It’s best suited for configuration tweaks, report changes, or process advice, not for break/fix issues or new module installs.

Pre\-Pay blocks are valid for 12 months from purchase and are non\-refundable, offering flexibility and cost savings for ongoing improvements.
