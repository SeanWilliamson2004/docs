**[Zendesk Chat (formerly Zopim)](https://www.zopim.com/)****is a service which adds live chat to the** [**Codis website**](http://www.codis.co.uk/)**.** 

## Signing in

Only one person at a time can be logged in. If someone logs in when another user is already logged in, the first user will be logged out

You can log in by going to the [Zopim Dashboard](http://dashboard.zopim.com/) and entering the credentials.

## Logging details

You can log customer details during a chat or after a chat. 

### During:

1. Click onto Visitor List
2. Open chat request (or wait for a customer to start chatting to you)
3. Fill in details, Name and Email into the fields; and Company Name typed into notes

### After:

1. Click onto History
2. Click on the Conversation
3. Go on to User Info
4. Make any additions or amendments to the persons details
