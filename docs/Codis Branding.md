**This page will go through the guidelines of the Codis Branding for the Sales and Marketing team.** 

For detailed information on Codis Branding please look at the PDF [Branding](https://codislimited.sharepoint.com/sites/Wiki/Marketing/Marketing%20Wiki/Documents/Branding/Branding.pdf). 

## Titles, headings, taglines and content

Titles are usually in "Title Case" whereas headings, taglines and content are in "Sentence case". 

Taglines should always have correct spelling, grammar and phrasing, and should begin with a capital letter and end with a full stop. They should usually appear beneath a heading.
