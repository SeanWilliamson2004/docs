To enable third party integration for Sage 50 v21 and above, log in and go to tools  \> Activation \> Enable 3rd Party Integration. Then enter the following depending on the sage 50 version.  

| Version | Serial Number | Activation Key |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sage Accounts v21 | SAGESDO | IIYFDYJ |
| Sage Accounts v22 | SAGESDO | EAHDFBL |
| Sage Accounts v23 | SAGESDO | RCYLSWK |
| Sage Accounts v24 | SAGESDO | ZHIEKPK |
