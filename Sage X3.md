[Sage ERP X3](http://www.sage.co.uk/software-and-services/business-management-solutions/sage-x3) is Powerful, scalable,cloud or on\-premise business management software. Simpler, faster and more flexible than traditional ERP systems. Enterprise\-class business management functionality and best\-practice. 

## Reading list

To familiarise yourself with Sage X3, it is recommended that you read the following in order: 

### Organisational structure

1. Create Account core model, Ledgers \& Charts of Accounts Approximate time 1 hrs.
2. [Companies \& Sites and Assign Account core model to Company](Companies & Sites and Assign Account core model to Company.md) Approximate time: 2\-3 hrs.
3. [Creation of Sage X3 GL Accounts](Creation of Sage X3 GL Accounts.md) Approximate time: 2\-3 hrs.
4. [Configuration of fiscal year and periods](Configuration of fiscal year and periods.md) Approximate time: 30 mins.
5. [Creation of Dimension types \& Dimensions](Creation of Dimension types & Dimensions.md) Approximate time: 1 hrs.

### Financials

1. [Creation of Customer \& Supplier master data](Creation of Customer & Supplier master data.md) Approx. time: 1\-2 hrs.
2. [Assigning specific GL Accounts in Accounting Codes Setup](Assigning specific GL Accounts in Accounting Codes Setup.md) Approx. time: 1\-2 hrs.
3. [Creation of supplier invoice and verifying the journal entries](Creation of supplier invoice and verifying the journal entries.md) Approx. time: 1 hrs.
4. Activating Batch server and executing Accounting tasks Approx. time: 15\-30 mins.
5. Execution of Invoices enquiry Approx. time: 15\-30 mins.
6. Payments for the Supplier Invoice and verifying journal entries Approx. time: 30 mins.

### Sales

1. [Sales Invoice Creation And Verifying Journal Entries](Sales Invoice Creation And Verifying Journal Entries.md) Approx. time: 30 mins.
