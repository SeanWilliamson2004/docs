In order to streamline the order process between ourselves
and Sage, avoid any unnecessary confusion and ensure that we are offering you
the best service possible Sage will now be asking for the below information to be
included on **all** Sage 200, Sage CRM, Sage 300 and Sage 50 Manufacturing
orders. 

**Existing Customers**


- The customers company
 name
- Their Sage serial number
 (For Sage CRM Standalone orders, please supply the Customers Account
 Number. We do not issue Sage CRM serial numbers in the way we do with Sage
 200\)
- Your Business Partner
 Account Number.



If an order for additional attributes is received without
the above information, the order will be sent back to Codis. 



**New Business Sales**


- Full details for the
 customer (Company name, full address, contact name, phone number and email
 address)
- Your Business Partner
 Account Number.



If an order is placed for a new business Sage 200 sale and
we have not included all of the above information, the order will be sent back
to Codis. 





Please continue to include a breakdown of what
we are ordering and the applicable costs where possible as this will also
ensure that our orders are actioned quickly and without confusion.
