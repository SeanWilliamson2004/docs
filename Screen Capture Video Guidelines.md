## Capturing the Video

1. Screen can be captured using the Snag\-It tool.
2. Capture the entire screen, ideally widescreen.
3. Close ALL Windows notifications when recording. E.g. no "you have 1 new email" pop\-ups during recording.
4. Hide the connection bar and the Snag\-It tool from the screen at all times.
5. Do not reveal your Desktop during recording. Video should begin and end with either Excel or Sage on the screen.
6. Don't move the mouse around too quickly.
7. Where necessary scroll through to show all the data. For example if you have downloaded customers from Sage into Excel, scroll down Excel and scroll back up to show all the customers have downloaded.
8. Where necessary expand columns in Excel so the column names appear in full. It is okay to record yourself expanding these columns.
9. Video should be around 3\-4 minutes long. Don't worry if a little over, as can edit if necessary.

## Script

1. Once the video is captured, write down the script for the video.
2. At the beginning state what the video is about. E.g. "In this tutorial, we look at how to quickly prepare and post monthly payroll data into Sage 200\."
3. End with: "Thank you for watching. Please remember to click the like button below and subscribe to our channel. If you have any questions or comments please leave those in the comments section below. For more information visit our website at codis.co.uk."

## Audio Recording

1. Record each step as separate audio files.
2. Speak loud and clear into the mic. Not too fast or not too slow.
