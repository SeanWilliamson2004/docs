# How to update the dashboards on Power BI workspace

1. There are two workspaces for every Live Customer/Tester. One of them is "Test" another is "Production".   
Go to the Test workspace.  
![Test workspace.png](images/PublishingImages_Pages_Dashboard_Update_Test_workspace.png)
2. Click on Source Control.  
![Source control.png](images/PublishingImages_Pages_Dashboard_Update_Source_control.png)  
Wait until it loads.
3. You will see two sections:  
1\)Changes: If you found anything in it. press "Undo All"  
2\)Updates: If you see there some things. press "Update All"  
![Source control on load.png](images/PublishingImages_Pages_Dashboard_Update_Source_control_on_load.png)  
Let it complete, it may take couple of minutes.
4. Go to "View Deployment Pipeline"  
![Dep pipeline.png](images/PublishingImages_Pages_Dashboard_Update_Dep_pipeline.png)
5. Press "Deploy to Production" on Test Workspace Section.  
![Deploy to pro.png](images/PublishingImages_Pages_Dashboard_Update_Deploy_to_pro.png)
6. Untick the box and press "Deploy"  
![Deploy.png](images/PublishingImages_Pages_Dashboard_Update_Deploy.png)  
Wait it to complete.
7. Press the reload button on production side near data set and Let it finish.  
![Done.png](images/PublishingImages_Pages_Dashboard_Update_Done.png)  
![reload.png](images/PublishingImages_Pages_Dashboard_Update_reload.png)  
The process to update the Dashboard is completed now.
