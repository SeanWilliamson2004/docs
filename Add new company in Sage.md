**Request for a New Sage Company Licence** 

If a Codis customer request an additional Sage company licence, here are the steps that needs to be followed.

(1\) Add opportunity.

(2\) **Get the quote from Sage**: When the customer request for an additional Sage licence. We need to send an email to Sage asking for the quote (providing the customer name and Sage serial number).

(3\) Quote received from Sage.

 (4\) **Prepare the Sage 50 Order:** Add our margin and send the **Sage 50 sales order** to the customer and request their signature. Normally the consultancy days are also involved, please seek advise from 

Codis support or Sales.

(5\) **Signed order / PO :** Once the customer approves the sales order, send the invoice out ( normal payment term is upon invoice and services upon delivery). 

(6\) Add a **consultancy case** if the consultancy days were agreed on the Sales order.

(7\) **Request new company name from the customer:** We need the name of the New Company and Registration Number in order to fill in the Extra GL form( copy of the GL form is on wiki ).

(8\) Fill in the **GL form**send it to **Sage** and request the licence string.

(9\) Once you get the licence string from Sage forward it to internal Support team and that needs to be filed in CRM.

(10\) **Invoice payment:** Check with accounts whether the invoice has been settled. The string needs to go out **only** if the payment has been settled. 

(11\) **Purchase Order** : Prepare the PO on Sage 50 and send the scan copy to Sage.
