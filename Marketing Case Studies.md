# Marketing Case Studies

  
 

## Proposal for Page Layout

- The top of the page contains the case study's company logo.
- This is followed by a powerful testimonial.
- Introduction: About the company and what they do.
- Problem: The challenges they faced, the issues they had without Excelerator
- Resolution: How Excelerator solved their problem.
- Results: What have been the ongoing results/benefits since using Excelerator.
- Talking head video for each of the above sections. I want customers to be able to follow the story just by watching the clips, if they don't read the text.
- In Summary: Visual icons \+ text that (ideally) provides some powerful stats about the impact of Excelerator.
- Try it Yourself: End the page with the push to get them to book a seminar.

  
Sample page on SquareSpace: [https://caterpillar\-tulip\-78l5\.squarespace.com/case\-study](https://caterpillar-tulip-78l5.squarespace.com/case-study)
