**This page describes the functionality and benefits of using CRM.** 

## Benefits

1\.1\) Check whether particular Company is being suspended or not before logging case.   
1\.2\) Log a New Case   
1\.3\) Update the Status of the Case that is Case is Awaiting for Customer to Accept or Close the Case.   
1\.4\) Also helps in maintain communication, task or files attached with particular logged case.   
1\.5\) Search for the existing company and contact details of the person in that company   
1\.6\) It can also help to Search the solution that is already exist for certain errors.   
1\.7\) You can also attach a solution to a particular case as well.   
1\.8\) It can also helps to register New Opportunity   
1\.9\) It provides hands on training regarding different features available in CRM.
